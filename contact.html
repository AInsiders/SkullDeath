Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my order?

+

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can also track your order by logging into your account and visiting the order history section.

What's your return policy?

+

We offer a 30-day return policy for all unworn items with original tags attached. Returns are free for US customers. International returns are subject to shipping costs.

When do new drops happen?

+

New drops happen every month, usually on the first Friday. Limited edition drops are announced on our social media channels and through our newsletter.

Do you ship internationally?

+

Yes, we ship to over 100 countries worldwide. International shipping typically takes 7-14 business days. All customs duties are the responsibility of the customer.

How do I join the elite community?

+

Join our newsletter and follow us on social media to stay connected with the SkullDeath community. You'll get early access to drops and exclusive content.

Are your products sustainable?

+

We're committed to sustainability and use eco-friendly materials where possible. All our packaging is recyclable, and we're working towards becoming carbon neutral.
